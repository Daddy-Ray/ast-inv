About AST Investing of Hong Kong

Since 2012, the company has pursued diversified investment and industrial operations, and has developed a growth framework centered on international investment and capital appreciation. Guided by long-term value creation, we continue to expand asset scale and operating quality across regional markets through prudent capital allocation and professional management.

On the investment side, we focus on cross-regional capital deployment and industrial synergy. Through project screening, post-investment management, and disciplined risk control, we continuously improve capital efficiency and global asset allocation capability.

In the construction segment, we specialize in sports and public venues, residential projects, and road infrastructure. Backed by mature project management capabilities and strong engineering execution, we deliver systematic construction and handover services for different regional markets.

In the mining segment, the company has built an integrated capability chain across copper exploration, mining, concentration, and smelting, enabling vertical coordination from resource identification to product delivery. The product portfolio includes copper concentrate, cathode copper, gold, silver, and sulfuric acid. In terms of scale, copper concentrate output ranks 20th globally, blister copper and cathode copper output rank 12th globally, and silver production accounted for about 51% of Kazakhstan's national total in 2020.

With Almaty, Kazakhstan as our primary operating center, we are also advancing international expansion. Our business has extended to the Gulf region, Romania, Hong Kong, Mainland China, and France, forming a cross-regional and multi-market collaborative network.

Looking ahead, we will continue to uphold prudent and disciplined investment principles, deepen synergy between industry and capital, optimize global asset allocation, and continuously enhance long-term returns and shareholder value.
