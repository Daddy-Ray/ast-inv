Investment Capabilities and Service Framework

Following international institutional standards, we present AST's value through track capabilities, client segments and execution process.

Ports and Logistics Infrastructure

We focus on equity investments, M&A integration and operating optimization for commercial ports and logistics nodes, with strong emphasis on cash flow resilience.

Resource Project Operations

We manage industrial mineral and precious metals projects across the full lifecycle, from assessment and development to ramp-up and governance enhancement.

Real Estate Investment and Development

We invest in regions supported by population and industry fundamentals, balancing commercial and residential demand with long-term value creation.

Institutional Solutions

For institutional clients, we provide allocation support, project screening and co-investment structures under clear risk-budget discipline.

Family Office Services

We support family capital with cross-generational planning, portfolio layering and liquidity management to balance growth and preservation.

Research and Execution Process

Our standard process includes opportunity screening, due diligence, investment committee review, post-investment monitoring and quarterly review cycles.
