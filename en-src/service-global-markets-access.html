Global Markets Access Cases

This page presents cross-regional execution cases focused on market entry, coordinated delivery, and long-term operating value.

Supported an Urban Mixed-Use Complex Development and Operations Program

This project is positioned as an integrated urban community combining residential units, commercial facilities, and public spaces. Facing complex planning constraints, extreme climate conditions, and multi-function development requirements, we provided end-to-end advisory support covering project planning, planning optimization, development strategy, and commercial positioning.

Client Challenge

In a multi-building mixed development with residential and public-commercial functions, the project needed to solve core issues across positioning, phasing, function coordination, and long-term operational sustainability.

Our Approach

We applied an integrated method of market research, planning optimization, commercial positioning, and technical implementation to rebuild the project's investment logic and product system around urban trends and local demand.

Key Actions

We delivered development model and phasing recommendations, optimized building massing and spatial structure, and refined residential-commercial functional layout. We also designed podium-level amenities. Considering local climate and structural loads, we proposed a hybrid implementation strategy to balance safety, efficiency, and schedule.

Results and Impact

The project established a clear development positioning and executable product structure, forming a coordinated residential-commercial-public-space community model and improving long-term operating value.

Integrated Mining Supply Chain Configuration and Operations Optimization

Centered on copper resource development and smelting systems, we designed an integrated supply chain configuration for the client. By linking steel, coal, iron ore, and pipe businesses, we built a cross-segment resource allocation and capacity coordination mechanism to strengthen raw-material security and delivery stability.

Core Business Chain

Covered exploration and mining, concentration and smelting, and key metal and derivative outputs to form a vertically integrated chain from raw inputs to finished products.

Production Areas and Facility Layout

Coordinated mines, concentrators, copper smelters, CHP units, and supporting manufacturing assets across Zhezkazgan, Karaganda, and Balkhash to optimize regional specialization and logistics routes.

Capacity and Supply Chain Configuration

Integrated copper, steel, coal, iron ore, and pipe businesses through a linked resource-production-market model to improve capacity utilization and multi-product collaboration efficiency.

Scale and Market Impact

Leveraged output strengths together with export channels and modernization plans to improve supply-chain resilience and long-term growth potential.
